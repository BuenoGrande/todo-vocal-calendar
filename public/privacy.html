Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Voice Todo Calendar ("the App") is a browser-based productivity tool that helps you manage tasks and schedule them on your Google Calendar using voice input.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its own servers. All data stays in your browser.

  • OpenAI API Key – Stored in your browser's local storage. Sent directly from your browser to OpenAI's API for voice transcription and task parsing. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Voice recordings – Audio is captured by your browser's microphone and sent directly to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. Recordings are not stored.
  • Google Calendar data – When you connect your Google account, the App reads and writes calendar events via the Google Calendar API. This data is processed entirely in your browser and is not stored on or sent to any third-party server other than Google.

Google API Services

The App uses Google OAuth 2.0 to access your Google Calendar. The App requests the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar scope to read and create calendar events. The App's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI – Voice audio and text are sent to OpenAI for processing. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Google – Calendar data is exchanged with Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app settings (API keys, preferences) are stored in your browser's local storage. No data is stored on external servers operated by the App. Clearing your browser data will remove all stored settings.

Cookies

The App does not use cookies.
